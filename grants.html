Available Grants

  • Internship Grants

    • Internship grants are financial awards that help cover expenses for students or graduates in unpaid or low-paid internships, allowing them to gain work experience without financial strain.



  • Bursaries

    • Bursaries are financial awards given to students based on financial need. They help cover education-related expenses such as tuition, books, and living costs, making education more accessible for those with limited financial resources.

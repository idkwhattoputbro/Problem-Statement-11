Available Scholarships

  • School-Based Scholarship

    A school-based scholarship is financial aid given by a school to its students. It helps attract talented students, reward good grades, and support those needing financial help. These scholarships can be based on academic merit, financial need, or other criteria like extracurricular activities


  • External-Based Scholarship

    Financial awards from organizations outside the student's school. They can be based on academic achievement, financial need, community service, or specific talents.
